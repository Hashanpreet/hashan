Our Story

Candy Funhouse is more than just a store; we're a family-owned business with a rich history of satisfying candy cravings. What started as a humble storefront in 2013 transformed into a thriving online hub, bridging the gap between candy lovers and their favorite treats. Our transition from brick-and-mortar to a virtual storefront in 2018 was a natural evolution, allowing us to reach candy enthusiasts far and wide with just a click. In 2023, NBA superstar Giannis Antetokounmpo and the Antetokounmpo Family joined the Candy Funhouse team as part owners and investors, bringing their passion for excellence and innovation to our candy empire. Join us in the celebration of all things sweet and pop-culture related!
